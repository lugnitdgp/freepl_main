This is a Fantasy Cricket League. We will be hosting matches mirroring the matches that are taking place live around the world. All you have to do is to select the team you would choose amongst the 22+ players participating in the match. You would be then allotted points on the basis of how the players actually performed

You have to select a minimum of 4 Batsmen, 2 Bowlers, 2 All Rounders and exactly 1 wicketkeeper. You must also choose a team captain, whose end points will be doubled.

THE POINTS WILL BE UPDATED WITHIN 3 HOURS OF COMPLETION OF THE MATCH.

SQUAD WILL BE LOCKED ONE AND A HALF HOUR BEFORE THE START OF THE MATCH.

Batting Points

Base Score 1 point per run.
Impact Score 2 points per six, -5 for a duck.
Milestone Score 10 points for every 25 runs scored.
Pace Bonus Runs Scored – Balls faced.(If negative then ignored)

Bowling Points

Base Score 20 points for first wicket taken.
Impact Score 20 points for a maiden over.
Milestone Score 10 points for each subsequent wicket.
Pace Bonus Balls bowled – Runs conceded. If positive, then it is doubled.(If negative then ignored)

Fielding Points

Catch 10 points
Stumping 15 points
Run Out 10 points for each player involved.

Bonus Points

Player of the Match 25 points
THE POINTS OF YOUR CAPTAIN GET DOUBLED
